Taverna 3 update site

This is the update and plugin site for Taverna 3.

Updates and plugins are available in three channels:

Stable

The 'stable' channels provide the official updates and plugins, verified by the myGrid team. 
http://updates.taverna.org.uk/workbench/3.0/stable/
http://updates.taverna.org.uk/workbench/3.0/stable/plugins/
http://updates.taverna.org.uk/commandline/3.0/stable/
http://updates.taverna.org.uk/commandline/3.0/stable/plugins/

Beta

The 'beta' channels provide early access to updates and plugins that are being tested before progressing to the 'stable' channel. 
http://updates.taverna.org.uk/workbench/3.0/beta/
http://updates.taverna.org.uk/workbench/3.0/beta/plugins/
http://updates.taverna.org.uk/commandline/3.0/beta/
http://updates.taverna.org.uk/commandline/3.0/beta/plugins/

Developer

The 'dev' channels provide the latest development from the nightly snapshot builds. Products on this channel might change at any time, and could occassionally not work. 
http://updates.taverna.org.uk/workbench/3.0/dev/
http://updates.taverna.org.uk/workbench/3.0/dev/plugins/
http://updates.taverna.org.uk/commandline/3.0/dev/
http://updates.taverna.org.uk/commandline/3.0/dev/plugins/

Adding your plugin

Third-party plugins are welcome to be listed on Taverna 3's plugin site, as long as they are publically available under a permissive (and ideally open source) license. In order to add your plugin, please contact support@mygrid.org.uk for further details.
